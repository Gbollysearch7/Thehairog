Week One Focus

Foundations & Closure Wig Construction

01

Day One

Orientation & Sewing Machine Basics

  • Class orientation and course overview
  • Introduction to wig making tools and materials
  • Understanding wig caps and wig sizing
  • Taking accurate head measurements (wig size charts)
  • Transferring measurements to wig caps
  • Cap adjustments for a proper and secure fit
  • Introduction to sewing machine parts, setup, and handling
02

Day Two

Stitch Practice & Cap Preparation

  • Practicing straight and zigzag stitches on fabric sheets
  • Machine control and stitch consistency practice
  • Drawing neat sewing guidelines on adjusted wig caps
  • Understanding track placement: spacing, density, and neat finishing
  • Introduction to closure wigs and closure types (2×6, 4×4, 5×5 etc.)
  • Hands-on practice: attaching 2–3 tracks together (double & triple wefting)
03

Day Three

Closure Wig Construction

  • Proper closure placement for a natural look
  • Securing tracks neatly from the back to the top
  • The flip-over sewing method for neat finishing
  • Step-by-step construction of a closure wig using zigzag stitch
  • Avoiding common wigging mistakes
  • Guided student practice session
04

Day Four

Closure Wig Completion & Corrections

  • Students complete their closure wigs
  • Instructor supervision and corrections
  • Neat finishing techniques
  • Troubleshooting construction mistakes
  • Professional tips for durability and comfort ✦
