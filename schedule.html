Day One

Orientation & Sewing Basics

  • Class orientation and programme overview

  • Taking accurate head measurements & wig size charts

Stitch Practice & Closure Prep

  • Straight and zigzag stitch practice on fabric sheets

Frontal Wig Making

  • Understanding frontal placement — normal & glueless methods
  • Guidelines and cap adjustments for frontal wigging
  • Step-by-step sewing of a frontal wig with neat finishing
  • Supervised practice session
  • Tips for creating natural-looking hairlines

Day Five

Review & Troubleshooting
