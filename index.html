Our Story

Built on one belief.

The Curriculum

3 weeks. 12 days. One complete
transformation.

An intensive, hands-on masterclass designed to take you from complete beginner to skilled wig maker and stylist — with real wigs in your hands from Day 1.

Week 1 · Days 1–4

Foundations & Closure Wig Construction

Sewing machine mastery, head measurements, cap adjustments, and closure wig construction from scratch. You will complete your first closure wig by Day 4.

Week 2 · Days 5–8

Frontal Wig Construction

Frontal placement techniques, glueless methods, and building your first frontal wig — with supervised practice to build speed and neatness.

Week 3 · Days 9–12

Styling, Lace Refill & Finishing

Heat and heatless curling, silk press, bob cutting, lace refill and repair, face-framing layers — culminating in your final project and certificate presentation.
