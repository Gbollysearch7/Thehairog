## 04. Content Pillars

All social media content must fall into one of these four pillars. Rotate through them every week.

| EDUCATION | PERSONAL BRAND | SOCIAL PROOF | SOFT SELL |
|---|---|---|---|
| *Builds authority* | *Builds trust* | *Builds desire* | *Drives enrollment* |
| Wig tips, tutorials, common mistakes, material guides, how-to breakdowns | Her story, opinions, day in the life, why she teaches, her values and standards | Student before/after results, testimonials, in-class moments, graduate stories | Cohort announcements, enrollment CTAs, payment plan reminders, countdown posts |

### Content Rule

**80 / 20 rule** — 80% of posts should educate, inspire, or build trust. Only 20% should sell directly. The audience must feel they are learning from her, not being sold to.

### Caption Formula (use on every post)

| Line | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Line 1** | Hook — say something bold, surprising, or relatable in one sentence |
| **Line 2–4** | Value or story — teach one thing or share one experience |
| **Line 5** | Soft CTA — "Comment YES if this helped" or "Save this for later" |
| **Line 6** | Enrollment CTA — "DM me LEARN to get class details for April" |
